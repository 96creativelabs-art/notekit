Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Notekit for free?

Yes! The free tier includes 20 notes, 5 projects, and all core features. Perfect for getting started.

What happens if I cancel Premium?

You'll revert to the free tier. Your existing notes remain, but you'll be limited to 20 notes and 5 projects. Premium features will be disabled.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally in your browser. We don't collect, sync, or store your notes anywhere. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I try Premium before buying?

Yes! Premium includes a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial without being charged.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

How do I activate my license?

After purchasing, you'll receive a license key via email. You can activate it directly in the extension's settings.
