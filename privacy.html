Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Notekit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect your personal data. All your notes, projects, tasks, and other data are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API. We do not have access to your data.

3. Local Storage

All data created in Notekit is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Notes and note content
  • Projects and project settings
  • Tasks and reminders
  • Tags and categories
  • Attachments and links
  • Settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. License Verification

If you purchase a Premium license, we verify your license key through our secure API. This process involves:

  • Sending your license key to our verification server
  • Checking license validity with Lemon Squeezy
  • Storing license status locally (not your personal information)

We do not store or have access to your personal information during this process.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Lemon Squeezy: For payment processing and license management. Their privacy policy applies to payment transactions.
  • Chrome Web Store: For extension distribution. Google's privacy policy applies.

6. Analytics

We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect usage statistics or user behavior data.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for:

  • Backing up your data regularly
  • Protecting your device from unauthorized access
  • Using the export feature to create backups

Premium users can encrypt notes with passwords for additional security.

8. Children's Privacy

Notekit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@notekit.app.
